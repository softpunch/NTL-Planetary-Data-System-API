COMET HALLEY ARCHIVE - INFRARED PHOTOMETRY

Status: ARCHIVED

Abstract: Additional infrared photometry of Comet 1P/Halley, taken over the period 1985-12-12 to 1986-05-06 using telescopes at three observatories: The Mt. Lemmon Observing Facility (MLOF) 1.52m telescope; the University of Minnesota O'Brien Observatory (OBO) 76cm telescope; and the University of Wyoming Infrared Observatory (WIRO) 2.34m telescope. The data were obtained by R. Gehrz and E. Ney.
