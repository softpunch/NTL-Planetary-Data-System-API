In the above table, the columns are as follows:                               
                                                                              
Filter   First line is the nomical Peak Wavelength/FWHM in Angstroms.         
         Successive lines  give the manufacturer of the filters measured:     
                                                                              
           Mic. = MicroCoatings, Inc., of Burlington, Massachusetts           
           And. = Andover Corp of Lawrence, Massachusetts                     
           Barr = Barr Associates, Inc., of Westford, massachusetts           
                                                                              
Lambda   Mean wavelength of peak transmission                                 
                                                                              
Trans.   Peak transmission                                                    
                                                                              
Width    Full widths at 80%, 50%, 10% and 1% of peak transmission.  The       
         value in parentheses is the RMS scatter of this transmission         
         from filter to filter.                                               
                                                                              
Values of "---" are standing in the place of values omitted from the          
original table through obvious or presumed typographical error.
