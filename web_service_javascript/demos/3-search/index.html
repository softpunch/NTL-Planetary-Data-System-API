Demo 3: search operations

This demo shows how to perform search operations over PDS database

Press button 1 to search all entities with DEEP word in its names (searchEntities example)
Press button 2 to search datasets with SOHO word in its names (searchEntitiesByType example)
Press button 3 to search datasets with V1.0 in its names from the mission with id 1 (DEEP IMPACT) (searchEntitiesByType example)
Press button 4 to search datasets for target with TEMPEL in its name (searchDataSetsByCriteria example)
Press button 5 to search datasets that were created in current millenium (after year 2000) (searchDataSetsByCriteria example)
